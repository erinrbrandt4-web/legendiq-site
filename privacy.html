LegendIQ

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 2026

LegendIQ is developed independently by Erin Brandt Motorsports. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

LegendIQ does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All setup sheets, preferences, and entered values are saved locally on your device only and are never sent anywhere.

Subscriptions

If you purchase a LegendIQ subscription, payment is processed entirely by Google Play. Erin Brandt Motorsports does not receive or store your payment details. Subscription management (cancellation, renewal, billing history) is handled through your Google Play account settings.

Analytics & Advertising

LegendIQ does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. No behavioral data is collected or shared.

Permissions

LegendIQ does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device sensor. The app only uses internet access to connect to Google Play Billing to verify and restore subscriptions.

Children

LegendIQ is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
